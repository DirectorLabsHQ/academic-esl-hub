Evidence-Informed English Assessment

Academic ESL Hub is designed for educational self-assessment and classroom support. Tools are informed by established frameworks such as the CEFR, Academic Word List (AWL), and published exam scaling data.

Results are estimates intended for learning and planning purposes. For official certification, consult accredited testing organizations.
