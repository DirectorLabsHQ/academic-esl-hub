Reading Benchmarks

WPM (Words Per Minute) is the gold standard for measuring reading fluency. For international students preparing for the IELTS or TOEFL, speed is just as critical as accuracy. A typical academic text requires a speed of at least 200 WPM to finish the reading section with enough time for the questions.

Practicing in our WPM Lab can help identify areas for improvement, track progress, and increase reading efficiency over time.

Improvement Strategies

To increase your WPM, avoid "back-skipping" (reading the same sentence twice) and sub-vocalization (saying the words in your head). Focus on expanding your peripheral vision to take in blocks of 3-4 words at once.

Consistent practice in our WPM Lab has been shown to increase processing speed by up to 25% in just 30 days.
