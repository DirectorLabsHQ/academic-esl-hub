Research & Methodology

The Academic ESL Hub is a project designed to operationalize modern SLA (Second Language Acquisition) research into practical, accessible tools for advanced learners.

Unlike general-purpose language apps, this suite is calibrated to the specific linguistic demands of post-secondary research and international standardized testing (IELTS/TOEFL).

01. Cognitive Processing

By analyzing Words Per Minute (WPM) alongside comprehension accuracy, we identify the "fluency-comprehension" balance necessary for academic success.

02. Lexical Mastery

Our diagnostics prioritize the Academic Word List (AWL), focusing on the abstract, high-utility vocabulary that differentiates C1/C2 mastery from intermediate levels.
