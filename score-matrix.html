What This Level Means

Your CEFR level represents your overall ability to use English in academic and professional contexts. It reflects how well you understand texts, express ideas, and perform under time constraints.

This alignment is based on widely accepted reference ranges between major English proficiency exams. Individual skill strengths may vary.

How to Improve from Here

  • Increase reading speed: practice timed reading with comprehension checks.
