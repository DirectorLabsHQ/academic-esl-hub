The CEFR Global Standard

The Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) is the international benchmark for describing language ability. It provides a common basis for the development of language syllabuses and curriculum guidelines across the globe.

Our Score Matrix provides a bridge between commercial exams and academic frameworks, ensuring a precise understanding of a candidate's true linguistic range.
