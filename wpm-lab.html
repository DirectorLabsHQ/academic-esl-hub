Why Measure Reading Speed?

Reading speed is a key factor in academic success. ESL students often struggle with comprehension when reading under time constraints. Our WPM Lab helps quantify reading fluency and comprehension together.

By understanding your reading pace, you can adjust your study strategy and improve test readiness for IELTS, TOEFL, and academic coursework.

Our Methodology

All passages are drawn from research-informed academic sources. Comprehension questions are designed to test understanding of key ideas and details. Accuracy and timing are combined to provide a reliable measure of reading fluency.
