How to improve your speed

The secret to reading faster is chunking. Instead of looking at every single word, try to see groups of 3-4 words at once. This reduces the strain on your eyes and helps your brain process complete ideas faster.

A good target for academic reading is 200+ WPM with 80% comprehension. If your speed is high but your quiz score is low, try slowing down and focusing on key words like however, therefore, or furthermore that show the logic of the story.

Did you know?

For ESL students, the biggest barrier to speed is vocabulary. If you don't know 95% of the words in a text, your speed will drop because you have to stop and think about each word. Use our Vocabulary Test in the menu to check your level!
